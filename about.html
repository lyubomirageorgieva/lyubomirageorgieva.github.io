About The Happy Restaurant



History

The first Happy Bar & Grill restaurant opened on Christmas Eve, on 24th December 1994 in the sea capital of Bulgaria – Varna, under the name Happy Seaman’s bar. The restaurant enjoyed great interest from the citizens of Varna, and a short time later, on 06th July 1995, opened the second establishment, Happy Orbit, which set the start of the largest and fastest growing chain of casual restaurants in the country. At present our company develops and manages 22 restaurants in Bulgaria and 2 in Barcelona, Spain.

Mission

Happy Bar & Grill is the most favourite brand in the catering sector in Bulgaria. Our mission is to bring joy and smile in the hectic lifestyle of our customers, offering them delicious and healthy meals, prepared and served with care, quick and quality service in a casual and pleasant atmosphere. Our 20 years of successful practice in this rapidly evolving business sector have shown that the CARE FOR THE CUSTOMER and their desires is what guides us in our daily work, aimed at improving the food quality and diversifying it, offering perfect service and creating a unique HAPPY atmosphere.

Brand

Happy Bar & Grill is a chain of casual restaurants in Bulgaria and Barcelona. Thanks to the exceptional quality of the food and high service standards, the Happy Bar & Grill brand is the strongest and the largest of its class.
